Information Gathering:

In a broad sense, we gather Personal Information, which may encompass Sensitive Data and other details. "Personal Information" refers to information that can identify and contact a specific individual. Sensitive Data includes optional information (such as photos, profiles, and lifestyle details) that could unveil aspects like ethnic origin, nationality, religion, or sexual orientation. When you share Sensitive Data with us, you are giving consent for its collection, use, and disclosure as per relevant privacy laws. Geolocation information may also be collected with your consent. This data collection can occur through websites, mobile applications, or other online services. By using the Service, you grant us permission to collect, analyze, and store data related to the Service. If you provide personal information through our Service, it will be transmitted to servers in the North America.

  • Upon creating an account, you supply us with essential information such as your phone number, email address, gender, date of birth, ethnic origin, nationality, religion, marital status, interest, personality and basic lifestyle information.

Profile Completion:
  • When completing your profile, you give consent for us to use the provided information as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
