Effective Date: 01,March 2025

At Darling, we are committed to ensuring the safety of all users, particularly children, from any form of sexual abuse, exploitation, or harm. We strictly enforce policies to prevent, detect, and take action against any content or behavior violating child safety laws.

1. Prohibited Content & Activities

  • - We DO NOT allow any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers minors.
  • - Any user engaging in grooming, harassment, or inappropriate interactions with minors will be permanently banned and reported to relevant authorities.
  • - We strictly prohibit the sharing of inappropriate images, videos, or text involving minors.

2. Proactive Moderation & Reporting

  • - We have a dedicated moderation team that manually verifies all profiles before approval to ensure compliance with our safety standards.
  • - In the future, we plan to implement AI-driven monitoring systems alongside human moderation to enhance our detection of policy violations.
  • - Users can report any suspicious activity within the app, and our team will investigate promptly.
  • - We work closely with law enforcement agencies to take action against any user engaging in CSAE-related violations.
